SalesRoleplay

Sales reps get unlimited practice calls against AI buyers, with structured scoring on every session, no manager time required. I designed the brand system, the marketing site that sells that idea, and its free-tool growth engine.

01 / The brief
"Roleplay training" sounds awkward. The brand had to make practice feel aspirational.
Sales leaders know reps improve by practicing, but every existing tool looks like corporate LMS software. The design direction pairs an editorial serif with a violet italic accent and puts the AI buyer personas, the product's real magic, front and center, orbiting the hero like characters you're about to meet.
02 / Brand system
Editorial serif, violet accent, persona orbit.
The hero typography leads with a serif display and a violet italic emphasis; navigation carries mega-menus and dual CTAs. One voice from the first headline to the footer sitemap.

Free AI tools that earn the signup.
